MARVEL Combat

The object of the game is to defeat all of your opponents before your health points drop to zero.


How to play:
  • First select the character that you want to be from the characters list, then select your opponent.
  • Press the attack button to begin combat.
  • If your health level drops to zero points you have lost the match and the game will rest.
  • If you choose your opponents wisely and beath them all before losing all of your health points.

Good Luck!
